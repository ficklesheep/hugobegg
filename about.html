About

Hugo is an award-winning photographer based in Melbourne. His work expresses a curiosity for people, place and movement through a diverse range of photographic styles. When not behind the lens, Hugo enjoys playing the trumpet and life as an electronic engineering student.

Hugo's talent has been recognized globally, including winning The Royal Meteorological Society's Young Weather Photographer of the Year Award in 2019 and earning multiple accolades and shorlistings in the Young Amateur Photographer of the Year competition, where he claimed first and fourth place in the Architecture category in 2021, and fourth place in the Low Light category in 2023. He has also been featured in Australasia's Top Emerging Photographers rankings, with top-20 placements in the Architecture (2022) and Black & White (2024) categories.

Professionally, Hugo merges his passions for music and photography by collaborating with Melbourne's classical musicians and ensembles, capturing their artistry with a keen eye for detail and emotion. His expertise in portraiture and street photography also makes him a sought-after choice for weddings and events.
